About Us

At Bowl29, we offer flavorful fresh fusion comfort dishes served in a bowl. You can visit us at our Leonardtown storefront inside Shepherd's Old Field Market, and you can also find our food truck at select events and community stops.

We love to create new dishes based on seasonality and what inspires us, so keep a look out for our specials. We want to bring our food passion to you!
